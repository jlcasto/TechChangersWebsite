Join our Team

Be You is currently hiring a Program Supervisor/Volunteer Coordinator. We also seek to expand our Board.


Be You is a local non-profit organization, focusing on creating a diverse community of youth makers – technologists, leaders, and innovators – by providing all students, especially underrepresented and socioeconomically challenged groups with opportunities to discover and foster their passion for STEM, computing, and making.

If you would like to help, please consider becoming a volunteer or make a donation.
