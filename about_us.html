Howdy! I'm Uyen. I currently live and work in the heart of Baltimore as a Software Engineer for a startup called Fearless. I try to save the world, one line of code at a time. People find me to be an enthusiastic, self-motivated team player with excellent communication skills. For the past several years, I have assisted in design, development, integration, and deployment for application software of all sizes. I am fueled by my passion for understanding all aspects of software development. I consider myself a life-long student, eager to build on my knowledge and stay up to date with the latest technology in the industry.

I am a food, fitness, and music enthusiast. I love traveling, going to concerts, trying new restaurants and just in general am a fan of trying new things and being open to new experiences and cultures. I spend my little bits of free time teaching middle and high schoolers how to code, endlessly rewatching Friends on Netflix, and just trying to be a somewhat functional member of society.
